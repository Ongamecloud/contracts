# Privacy Policy

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best possible service, we may collect and store the following information:

- **Personal Information**: Your name, email address, country, and any other information provided during registration or account creation.

- **IP Address**: Your IP address is not directly logged by our service, but through its use and the use of our game servers, your IP address may be logged. If server logging is disabled, IP addresses are not stored.

---

## 2. Use of Your Information

At **Ongamecloud**, we are committed to using your data responsibly. Here's how we use it:

- **Provision of Service**: Your information is necessary to create and manage your account, as well as to ensure the proper functioning of our servers.

- **Updates and Communication:** We may send you a maximum of one email per month to inform you of important updates, new features, or changes to our services.

- **No Advertising:** We do not share or sell your information for advertising purposes. You will never receive marketing emails or advertisements from third parties through our services.

---

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

- **Performance of a Contract:** To provide the services you have requested.

- **Consent:** When you activate certain optional features.

- **Legitimate Interest:** To improve our services, ensure their security, and prevent misuse.

---

## 4. Use by Minors

Our services are **not intended for individuals under the age of 18** without the consent of a parent or legal guardian.

- If you are under 18, you must have explicit permission from a parent or guardian to use our services.

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover that a minor has provided us with data without parental consent, we will take the necessary steps to delete that information immediately.

- Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with data without authorization can contact us directly at **privacy@ongamecloud.com** or by mail at 61 RUE DE LYON 75012 PARIS, FRANCE.

---

## 5. Data Security

We implement rigorous security measures to protect your personal information, including:

- **Encrypted data transmission** between our servers and your device.

- **Limited access to your data**: only authorized individuals who have signed a non-disclosure agreement may access it. - **Regular review** of our systems and procedures to prevent unauthorized access, breaches, or misuse.

---

## 6. Data Retention Period

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services.
Technical logs are automatically deleted after a defined period, except in the case of a security investigation or legal obligation.
If you delete your account, your personal data will be erased from our systems within a reasonable timeframe, unless legally required to retain it.

---

## 7. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access and Rectification**: You can request access to your personal data or the correction of inaccurate data.

- **Data Deletion**: You can request the deletion of your personal data at any time. Some information may be retained to comply with legal obligations. You can delete your account directly from your dashboard or by contacting us at **privacy@ongamecloud.com**
- **Disabling Logging**: You have the option to disable logging on your servers, which prevents the logging of certain data such as your IP address.

---

## 8. Sharing with Third Parties

**Ongamecloud** does not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only disclose it in the following cases:

- To comply with legal obligations or enforce our Terms of Service.

- To protect the rights, property, or safety of OngameCloud, our users, or third parties.

- To provide and improve our services, we rely on several third-party services. In some cases, these services may require access to your personal information. We ensure that these partners adhere to strict privacy and security standards.

The listInformation on the third-party services used, as well as details on how they process your data, is available upon request via email, mail, or Discord ticket.

---

## 9. Data Hosting and Transfer

Our servers are located in France, Finland, Switzerland, and Germany.

If your data is transferred to another country, we ensure that appropriate measures are in place to guarantee its confidentiality and security, in accordance with international data protection standards.

---

## 10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons.

If so, we will notify you by email or through our platform.
