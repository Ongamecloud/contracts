## 2. Information We Collect

In order to provide our services, we may collect and store the following information:

- **Personal information**: your name, email address, country, and any other information provided during registration or account creation.
- **Payment data**: the banking information required to process your transactions is collected and processed directly by our payment provider Stripe. OGC does not store your banking details.
- **IP address**: your IP address is not directly recorded by our services. However, when using our game servers, your IP address may appear in the server's technical logs. If logging is disabled by the user, IP addresses are not stored.

## 3. Use of Your Information

At **Ongamecloud**, we are committed to using your data responsibly:

- **Provision of service**: your information is necessary to create and manage your account, as well as to ensure the proper functioning of our servers.
- **Updates and communication**: we may send you a maximum of one email per month to inform you of important updates, new features, or changes to our services.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases, in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR):

- **Performance of a contract** (Article 6.1.b): to provide the services you have subscribed to.
- **Consent** (Article 6.1.a): when you activate certain optional features, including the artificial intelligence agent.
- **Legitimate interest** (Article 6.1.f): to improve our services, ensure their security, and prevent misuse.
- **Legal obligation** (Article 6.1.c): to comply with our accounting and tax obligations (retention of billing data).

## 5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Ongamecloud **does not use any tracking, profiling or advertising cookies**.

We use only **strictly necessary cookies** for the operation of the platform, including:

- Session management and authentication.
- Maintaining your display and configuration preferences.
- The proper technical functioning of the platform.

These cookies are essential to the provision of the service and do not require your prior consent in accordance with Article 82 of the French Data Protection Act (Loi Informatique et Libertes). They do not collect any personal data for commercial purposes and are not shared with any third party.

## 6. Use by Minors

Our services are **not intended for individuals under the age of 18** without the consent of a parent or legal guardian.

In accordance with Article 45 of French Law No. 78-17 of 6 January 1978 (Data Protection Act), consent to the processing of personal data of a minor under **15 years of age** must be given by the holder of parental authority. Above 15 years of age, a minor may consent to the processing of their data; however, subscribing to a paid plan requires the legal capacity to enter into contracts (18 years of age or authorisation from a legal representative).

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors without the required consent. If we discover that a minor has provided us with data without appropriate consent, we will take the necessary steps to delete that information promptly.
- Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with data without authorisation can contact us at: **privacy@ongamecloud.com**, by ticket on our Discord community ([discord.gg/ZdQS93YPPb](https://discord.gg/ZdQS93YPPb)), or by postal mail at 61 Rue de Lyon, 75012 Paris, France.

## 7. Data Security

We implement technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

- **Encrypted transmissions**: data exchanges between our servers and your device are protected by encryption protocols (TLS/SSL).
- **Restricted access**: only authorised personnel who have signed a confidentiality agreement may access your personal data.
- **Regular audits**: our systems and procedures are reviewed regularly to prevent unauthorised access, breaches, or misuse.

---

## 8. Data Retention Period

We retain your personal data for the following periods:

- **Account data** (name, email, country): retained for the duration of your registration. If you delete your account, this data is erased within **30 days**, unless a legal obligation requires otherwise.
- **Billing data**: retained for **10 years** from the date of the transaction, in accordance with French accounting and tax obligations (Article L.123-22 of the French Commercial Code).
- **Technical logs**: automatically deleted after a maximum period of **12 months**, unless a security investigation is ongoing or a legal obligation requires their retention.
- **Support data** (tickets, correspondence): retained for **24 months** after the ticket is closed, for service monitoring and quality purposes.

## 9. Your Rights

In accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and the French Data Protection Act, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Right of access** (Article 15 GDPR): you may obtain confirmation as to whether your data is being processed and request a copy thereof.
- **Right to rectification** (Article 16): you may request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Right to erasure** (Article 17): you may request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations. You can delete your account directly from your dashboard or by contacting us at **privacy@ongamecloud.com**.
- **Right to restriction of processing** (Article 18): you may request the restriction of processing of your data in the circumstances provided for by the regulation.
- **Right to data portability** (Article 20): you may receive your data in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format, and transmit it to another controller.
- **Right to object** (Article 21): you may object to the processing of your data based on legitimate interest, on grounds relating to your particular situation.
- **Disabling logging**: you have the option to disable logging on your servers, which prevents the recording of certain data such as your IP address.
