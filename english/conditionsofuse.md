## 6. Referral Program

### 6.1. General Operation

Each OGC user automatically receives a unique referral link in the format:
`/refer/(simplified-username)-referralId`

This link allows you to invite new users to join the platform.

- **Token = €1**: Tokens can be used as a discount or payment method on the OGC platform.

- When someone creates an account via a referral link, the system records the information, but **no tokens are yet awarded**. - If the invited person **subscribes to a paid subscription**, then:

- The **referrer** receives **3 pending tokens**.

- The **referred person** also receives **3 pending tokens**.

- These tokens are **locked for 14 days** after the subscription payment date to prevent refunds.

- After this period, if the subscription has not been refunded, the pending tokens are **validated and transferred to each user's account**.
